============================
Welcome to Nutanix Workshops
============================

Are *you* one of the following:

* A prospective Nutanix customer looking to get hands on and don't know where to start?
* A current Nutanix customer who can't get enough?
* A Nutanix channel partner looking for training and understanding on how Nutanix can help your customers?
* A Nutanix employee wanting to keep your technical skills sharp?
* A random person on the Internet who isn't entirely sure how you wound up here?

If so, then you've come to the right place! Nutanix Workshops provides comprehensive hands-on-lab guides for implementing many solutions on top of the **Nutanix Enterprise Cloud**.
